Lasagna Recipe

Here's how to prepare a home-made lasagna

Lasagna is a rich, layered Italian dish made with sheets of pasta, a savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef (or intalian sausage)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzrella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta:Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain.
  2. Make the sauce:Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic, then add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and seasonings. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture:In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan.
  4. Layer the lasagna:
    • Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish.
    • Add noodles, then a layer of ricotta mixture.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella, then repeat the layers.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes until cheese is golden.
  6. Let it restAllow the lasagna to cool for 10 minutes before slicing.
